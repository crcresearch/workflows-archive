  • About

The Open Coalition for Research Workflows brings together experts from various science and engineering domains with computer scientists who share one common vision, that all researchers have access to high-quality workflow environments that meet their needs, and selecting from the available choices is easy with tradeoffs that are simple to understand.

Workshops

Through a series of workshops, we aim to bring together developers of workflow infrastructure, users and data producers to identify challenges and opportunities for enhancing workflow environments, including the documentation, software, tools and related resources. Please come and talk to us at the WORKS workshop at SC17

Future Proposals

We will look for funding opportunities, such as the former NIH call BD2K Support for Meetings of Data Science Related Organizations to support our efforts to bring currently siloed workflow communities together to discuss common needs and requirements.
